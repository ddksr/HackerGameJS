Get to know your tools
This is the first assignment. Your mission is to get to know the console.
Hello, Anon-chan!
I am your sensei. I will be your guide trough your exercises and also your menthor on your missions.
If you have any questions, just press HELP or HINT buttons. If you are ready, you can press START and read abbout your first assignment.
Type something in the terminal. The terminal is the big black screen with an blinking cursor located on your left. When you are finished typing press ENTER on your keyboard.
Make the terminal respond. Use the 'echo' command and try making the terminal respond 'Hello world!'. Try not to use hints because you will recieve penalty points. Type 'help echo' in the terminal. The 'help' command can help you understand any of the terminal commands you will ever need to use. Type 'echo Hello World' in the terminal.
Send a message to your sensei. The command is called 'sensei' and is simple to use. Use the 'help' command to learn how to use it and send a test message. The content of the message isn't important. Type 'help sensei' First type 'help sensei' and then 'sensei my message'
Almost every assignment is time limited. You should always check how much time you have left and what you can do to get some additional time. Check your time now and report the number of minutes to your sensei. The remaining time can be viewed in the lower left corner. Type 'sensei REMAINING TIME in seconds'
For every completed task you are scored depending on your succsess. Hints can lower your score but help commands and the help button don't. Now, report your current score to sensei. Current and best score are located at the bootom of the page. Current score is calculated with respect to your progress. Best score is your best achieved score for this task.
